Costco Butter Chicken

Description

This is a more recent addition to my cooking arsenal with the help of Costco. With their butter chicken mix, it is super easy to make delicious butter chicken in big batches. The biggest down side is that you might need a pretty large pot/pan to cook this in.

Ingredients

  • 1 jar of KFI Butter Chicken sauce
  • 2 pounds of boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 teaspoon of cooking oil

Instructions

  • Dice your chicken thighs into bite sized cubes, trimming away as much fat as possible.
  • Heat oil at medium-low and add chicken when hot.
  • Stir fry chicken until mostly cooked.
  • Reduce heat to low and add enough sauce such that the chicken is fully covered and mostly submerged.
  • Let simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring every 2 or 3 minutes.

Tips and Notes

  • You can use any part of the chicken for this, I've just found that boneless and skinless chicken thighs are the easiest to prep and taste the best.
  • Trimming away the fat is preference. I don't like excess fat on my chicken but it does add flavor and tenderness to the dish as a whole.
  • The sauce can end up a little mild due to the water content of the chicken and sauce. Before adding the sauce, I like to remove the excess water at the bottom of the pan. If I have time, I will let it simmer for about 30 minutes instead, as this will reduce the sauce, making it more concentrated.
